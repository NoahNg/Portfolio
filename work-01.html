001 : Godot Platformer 2D

This was made for an assignment, whose task was to create a game with a provided Sprite and everyone had a different theme. Mine was "Native Forest".

Player needs to collect 3 pies for each level to proceed to the next one. Some pies are protected with a puzzle or an enemy, and there are some bats that you can shoot with fireballs or just avoid them.
